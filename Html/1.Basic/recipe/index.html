Donut Recipe (Classic Fried Donuts)

Ingredients

  • ¼ teaspoons active dry yeast (1 packet)
  • ¾ cup warm milk (about 110°F / 43°C)
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour (plus extra for dusting)
  • Oil for frying (vegetable or canola)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, dissolve yeast in warm milk and let it sit for 5–10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Add sugar, melted butter, salt, egg, and flour. Mix until dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5–7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let rise in a warm place for 1–2 hours or until doubled in size.
  5. Roll out the dough to about ½ inch (1.25 cm) thick. Cut with a donut cutter (or round cutter plus a smaller one for the center).
  6. Place donuts on a tray, cover lightly, and let rise for 30–40 minutes.
  7. Heat oil in a deep pan to 350°F (175°C). Fry donuts for 1–2 minutes per side until golden brown.
  8. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  9. For glaze: whisk powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla until smooth. Dip warm donuts in glaze and let set.
